How does subscription work? - Premium

Signing the app ensures that you have access to all the traces of the app.

Monthly Mode

You will have access to all the traces during the period of 1 month, after which the traces will be locked again.

You will only be charged 1 time at the time of your signature, the new charge will be after 1 month.

Semiannual Modality

You will have access to all traces during the 6-month period, after which the traces will be locked again.

You will be charged only once at the time of your signature, the new charge will be after 6 months.

Subscription cancellation

Cancellation can be made at any time

I canceled after payment was made, will I lose access to the tracings?

No. You do not lose access to the tracings.

Traces will only be locked after the period you have chosen to end.

Example: I signed up for 6 months, but I canceled it in the first month. The access of the traces will stay for another 5 months and after will be blocked access.

To cancel

1- On your Android phone or tablet, open the Google Play Store.
2- Touch Menu Subscription.
3- Touch the signature you'd like to cancel.
4 - Touch Cancel subscription.
5 - Follow the instructions on the screen.

Important: Uninstalling an app will not automatically stop your subscription. You need to cancel the subscription to close it. If you uninstall the app without canceling the subscription, there will still be a charge. If an app you've signed is removed from Google Play, the future subscription will be automatically canceled, but the previous ones will not be refunded.

Restart or re-enable a subscription

If the subscription has been canceled but is still active:

1- On your Android phone or tablet, open the Google Play Store.
2- Touch Menu Subscription.
3- Touch the signature you want to re-enable.
4- Touch Reactivate.
5- Follow the instructions on the screen.
